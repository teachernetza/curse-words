Linguistic Analysis

The F-Word Grammar

The ultimate linguistic chameleon.

It is the only word in the English language that can be used as almost any part of speech. It is the ultimate intensifier.

1. As a Verb (To ruin / To have sex)
"Don't fuck with me." / "He fucked the project."
2. As an Exclamation (Frustration)
"Fuck! I stubbed my toe."
3. As a Noun (A person or an amount)
"He is a dumb fuck." / "I don't give a fuck."
4. As an Adjective (Descriptive)
"This is a fucking disaster."
5. As an Adverb (Intensifier = Very)
"That car is fucking fast."
6. As an Infix (Tmesis - Inserted INSIDE a word)
"Abso-fucking-lutely" / "Un-fucking-believable."
